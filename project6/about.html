Leo grew up in Concord, North Carolina, learning the art of baking from his grandfather. His passion for baking is what led him to open Sunflower Bakery, with a vision of creating delicious, high-quality treats made with love.

Amanda Anderson

Amanda has always had a love for the culinary arts. With a background in pastry arts, she brings her creativity and passion for flavor to every item on the menu. Amanda is committed to using only the finest ingredients, ensuring every bite is a delight.
