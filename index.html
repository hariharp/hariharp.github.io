How to Dress for North Carolina Weather!

North Carolina weather can be particulary difficult to understand. Especially in the transition period of summer to fall.
Mornings tend to be a bit chilly and even cold, while as it reaches mid day it can be quite hot.
Lucky for you, here are some tips from a North Carolina native!

  • Layer, layer, layer!
  • Chose versatile pieces that are breathable.
  • Bring an umbrella or rain jacket!
  • Accessorize smartly!
  • Stay comfortable and functional.

I hope those tips were helpful! But NC weather really isn't that bad, it's just a really good mix of everything!

Looking for good layering pieces?


Look no further, here are some of my favorite things to keep on hand!
  • A thin base layer that looks good on its own!
  • A nice lightweight sweater, jacket, or cardigan.
  • If it's really cold in the mornings and evenings keep a thicker jacket or cardigan on hand.
Even though these are just three pieces, it makes all the difference when trying to be comfortable in constantly
changing weather!

Looking for the right footwear?

If you are walking around a lot like me, here are some things to think about when choosing shoes! Things to look for:
  • Look for shoes with good cushioning, gel or memory foam are nice!
  • Good arch support is important, this helps in preventing foot pain.
  • Shoes made from breathable material can make all the difference.
  • A non-slip sole can help prevent slips and even provide your with stability
  • Flexible shoes that move with your feet are great, not stiff ones.
