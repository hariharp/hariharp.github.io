About Me

I’ve always been a creator at heart drawing and crafting new inventions daily since I was a child, now channeled into UI and UX. I enjoy bringing structure to ideas while still making them feel full of life. I’m inspired by my roots, and that shows up in how I design - warm, intentional, bold, and always thoughtful. I care deeply about how people feel when they interact with something. Empathy is at the heart of everything I do. To me, good design isn’t just about how it looks or functions it’s about whether it connects. Whether it makes sense, makes someone feel seen, or simply makes their day a little easier.
