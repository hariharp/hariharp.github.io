About My Work Reach Out

I’m a creative thinker with a passion for design, wellness, and storytelling. With a degree in New Media and Entrepreneurship from UNC-Chapel Hill, I enjoy exploring how technology and creativity intersect to shape meaningful experiences. Meditation and breathwork have played a transformative role in my life, inspiring me to share their benefits with others. I’m also an avid traveler, always seeking new perspectives—whether wandering the streets of Sausalito, hiking Yosemite, or immersing myself in the vibrant culture of Delhi. My journey is just beginning, and I’m excited to share my work, collaborate with like-minded creatives, and continue learning. Thanks for stopping by, I look forward to connecting!
