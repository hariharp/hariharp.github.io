About My Work Reach Out

Hello, I am Poorvi Hariharan

I am driven by curiosity and a deep passion for wellness, mental health, and the art of finding inner balance through meditation and breathwork. Breathwork and meditation hold great value in my life and I have dedicated a lot of my time to further spreading the knowledge of breathwork and meditation. Traveling the world is my biggest dream and favourite activity, from exploring the charming streets of Sausalito and the natural beauty of Yosemite to experiencing the vibrant culture of Delhi, India. I dream of visiting every corner of the globe to experience new perspectives, savor diverse cuisines, and form lasting connections. As a senior at UNC-CH majoring in Communication Studies with a New Media focus and a minor in Entrepreneurship, my education has become a foundation for my desire to make a meaningful impact. My journey is just beginning, and I am excited to share my work, collaborate with like-minded creatives, and connect with those who share my passion for art, wellness, and entrepreneurship. Thank you for visiting, and I look forward to seeing where our paths might cross.
